About Me

Aspiring PhD candidate with a DMD from Azerbaijan Medical University and a Bachelor's in Biomedical Engineering. I bring an interdisciplinary background that integrates clinical dentistry with biomedical innovation. Fluent in multiple languages and internationally recognized for scientific creativity, I am particularly interested in research that advances dental technologies, oral pathology, biomaterials, and AI-assisted diagnostics. I'm passionate about bridging the gap between clinical dentistry and cutting-edge biomedical engineering. My journey has taken me from designing robotic exoskeletons to researching AI-assisted diagnostics in dentistry. I believe that interdisciplinary approaches are key to solving complex medical challenges and improving patient outcomes.
